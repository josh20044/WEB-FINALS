Rise of the Norn Event


MAY 5, 2023






Only one Redemption dragon has ever managed to atone for her sins and redeem herself… She's been gifted a Heroic status in return. Meet the first-ever Heroic Redemption dragon: the High Redemption Norn Dragon.


From May 4th to July 13th, 2023, take part in the 70-day Rise of the Norn event to earn tons of rewards, including two Special Skill Skins:


- Fallen Skill Skin that deals boosted damage for the No Remorse Dream Skill of the new Heroic Redemption dragon.


- Risen Skill Skin that deals boosted damage for the Ice, Chaos & Wind attacks of the new Heroic Redemption dragon.



RISE OF THE NORN COLLECTION


Collect Redemption Insignias to complete the Rise of the Norn event.


For the whole duration of the event, Redemption Insignias can be found in different areas of the game:


- The next THREE Divine Pass Seasons;


1. Rise of the Norn - High Redemption Norn Dragon (May 4th - May 18th)


2. Chariot’s Chase - Arcana Chariot Dragon (May 18th - June 15th)


3. A Fool’s Game - Arcana Fool Dragon (June 15th - July 13th)


- Use your leftover Divine Points to claim extra Insignias at the end of the reward path


- All of the Event Islands until July 13th


- In Quests and Offers



REWARDS


Complete the Rise of the Norn event to earn tons of rewards.


Reach each collection milestone to earn rewards of increasing value:


- Milestone 1 “Collection Redemption Insignias” > Redemption Chest: obtain this Chest for a chance to earn Redemption Insignias, Gems, Orbs of the High Redemption Norn Dragon, Breeding, Health and Damage Perks, Food, and Joker Orbs;


- Milestone 2 “Rise for forgiveness” > Fallen Redemption Chest: obtain this Chest for a chance to earn Orbs of the High Redemption Norn Dragon, and Orbs of the most powerful dragons in the Dragonverse, as well as Food, Missing Dragon Rescue Keys, Joker Orbs, Trade Essences, and Kindergarten & Rainbow Tokens;


- Milestone 3 “Fall into the Ring” > Risen Redemption Chest: obtain this Chest for a chance to earn the High Redemption Norn Dragon herself or eggs and Orbs of the most powerful Dragons in the Dragonverse;


- Milestone 4 “Try out the Fallen Skin!” > Earn the special Fallen Skill Skin and 500 Orbs of the High Redemption Norn Dragon.


BIRTHDAY DIVINE PASS: RISE OF THE NORN

(May 4th - May 18th)


Play this special Season of this Divine Pass to obtain the first-ever Heroic Redemption dragon: the High Redemption Norn Dragon.


Additionally, by purchasing the Elite Pass, you’ll obtain 300 Orbs of the High Redemption Norn Dragon and her special Risen Skill Skin.


NEW SKILL SKINS


The Risen and Fallen Skill Skins will take your High Redemption Norn Dragon to the next level!


Risen Skill Skin:


By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage for her Ice, Chaos & Wind attacks!


Fallen Skill Skin:


By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage for her No Remorse Dream skill, reduce damage done to the user and the skill cooldown will be reduced by 1 turn!
