Ace of Arcana Event


OCTOBER 19, 2023






Even the darkest secrets of the most holy dragons will eventually be uncovered... Judgement Day is coming and there's nowhere to hide. Face your truths or feel the Arcana wrath


Meet the first-ever Heroic Arcana dragon: the High Arcana Judgement Dragon!


From October 19th to December 28th, 2023, participate in the 70-day Ace of Arcana event to earn amazing rewards, including two Special Skill Skins:


  • Fortune Skill Skin that boosts the High Arcana Judgement’s Arcana Absorption skill (both health and damage) and gives boosted damage to its Pure Attack!

  • Cursed Skill Skin that boosts the High Arcana Judgement’s Emergency skill and gives boosted damage to its Wind Attack!


ACE OF ARCANA COLLECTION


Collect Arcana Insignias to complete milestones in the Ace of Arcana event. For the whole duration of the event, Arcana Insignias can be found in:


  • The next THREE Divine Pass Seasons;

    • 1. Ace of Arcana - High Arcana Judgement Dragon (October 19th - November 2nd)


    2. Ghosted. - Quantum Ghost Dragon (November 2nd - November 30th)


    3. Gone in a Flash - Quantum Flash Dragon (November 30th - December 28th)


  • Gone in a Flash - Quantum Flash Dragon (November 30th - December 28th)

  • You can also find a new and exclusive offer in the Dragon City Webstore: the Elite Pass PLUS! Purchase the Elite Pass PLUS via the Webstore to receive even more rewards!

  • All of the Heroic Race, Grid, and Maze Event Islands from October 19th to December 25th

  • Official Dragon City Webstore

  • Quests for the Arcana Dragons and Offers


REWARDS


Participating in the Ace of Arcana event means you can get tons of rewards! These are the rewards waiting for you at each milestone:


- Milestone 1 “Collect Arcana Insignias” > Open this Arcana Chest for a chance to earn Gems, Orbs of Arcana Dragons, Arcana Insignias, Food, Breeding Perks, and Habitat Tokens;


- Milestone 2 “Deal your cards” > Open this Arcana Chest for a chance to earn Orbs of the High Arcana Judgement Dragon and Orbs of some of the most powerful dragons in Dragon City;


- Milestone 3 “Reveal your future” > Open this Arcana Chest to obtain a guaranteed High Arcana Judgement Dragon and for a chance to obtain some of the most powerful dragons in Dragon City;


- Milestone 4 “Try out the Fortune Skin!” > Open this Arcana Chest to obtain 500 Orbs of the High Arcana Judgement Dragon and its new, special Fortune Skill Skin!



SPECIAL DIVINE PASS: ACE OF ARCANA


(October 19th - November 2nd)


Play this special Season of the Divine Pass to obtain the first-ever Heroic Arcana dragon: the High Arcana Judgement Dragon.


Additionally, by purchasing the Elite Pass, you’ll obtain 300 Orbs of the High Arcana Judgement Dragon and its special Cursed Skill Skin.


And that’s not all… Treat yourself to the new Webstore-exclusive offer: the Elite Pass PLUS! By purchasing the Elite Pass PLUS, you will receive the Elite Pass, 50 Arcana Insignias, and 40 Gems in-game.



NEW SKILL SKINS


Take your High Arcana Judgement Dragon to the next level with the Fortune and Cursed Skill Skins!


- Fortune Skill Skin


By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage and receive boosted health for its Absorption skill, as well as deal boosted damage for its Pure attack.


Get the Fortune Skill Skin by completing the Ace of Arcana collection.


Please restart your game for this to take effect.


- Cursed Skill Skin


By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage for its Emergency skill and Wind attack.


Get the Cursed Skill Skin in the Elite Divine Pass during the Ace of Arcana Season.


Please restart your game for this to take effect.
