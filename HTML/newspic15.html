Berserker Dragons


DECEMBER 21, 2023






Did you really think the peace in Dragon City would last so long? Danger is always around the corner.


Driven by primal instincts and a lust to defeat, the Berserkers are coming to bring rage and chaos to your City.


Don’t make them go berserk, or you’ll find yourself dealing with the fatal consequences…


Meet the new Berserkers and try out their unique family skill!



BERSERK SKILL


Berserkers possess the special Berserk skill.


The Berserk skill is a passive skill that increases the damage dealt by the Berserker dragon. There are two (2) stages of damage increase:


1. When a Berserker knocks out an opponent dragon, its rage power is boosted, thus dealing more damage to its next opponent;


2. When the same Berserker knocks out a second opponent, its rage power is further boosted, thus dealing even more damage to its next opponent.

This boosted damage is reset at the end of every battle.


HOW DOES THE BERSERK SKILL INTERACT WITH THE PHOENIX OR REAPER PERKS?


  • If a Berserker knocks out an opponent dragon who’s equipped with the Phoenix Perk, then the Berserk skill is not activated.

  • The Berserk Skill is however activated if the Berserker is able to continue to knock out the opponent after its revival from the Phoenix Perk.

  • If a Berserker is equipped with the Reaper Perk, thus canceling the opponent dragon’s Phoenix Perk, the Berserk skill is activated.


HOW DOES THE BERSERK SKILL INTERACT WITH THE REINCARNATION SKILL?


  • When a Berserker knocks out an opponent dragon who has the passive Reincarnation skill, then the Berserk skill is not activated

  • When a Berserker knocks out an opponent dragon, and the Berserk Skill is activated, yet the knocked-out opponent is revived by another dragon’s Reincarnation ability, the Berserk skill continues to be in effect and is not deactivated.

  • When a Berserker knocks out an opponent dragon who was recently revived by another dragon’s Reincarnation skill, the Berserk Skill is activated.

  • When a Berserker, whose damage has been boosted thanks to its Berserk skill, is knocked out by an opponent dragon, but then later reincarnated by another dragon on its team, the Berserk skill is reset.


Remember, there are four (4) Berserkers to collect! Reunite all four members of this family in the upcoming months to obtain the 5th Mythical Berserker dragon for FREE!
