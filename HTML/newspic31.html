A ‘PERKY’ UPDATE!


JULY 24, 2023






The Grim Reaper comes bearing gifts… Check out this exciting update to your dragon Perks!


Here’s everything you need to know:



REAPER PERK


Equip your dragons with the brand-new Reaper Perk to damage enemy dragons that revive on the battlefield


When attached to your dragon, the Reaper Perk will target the revived opponent dragon and remove 3% of its maximum life points.


The Reaper Perk can be triggered multiple times but won’t be activated if the dragon it is attached to is on the bench.


Just like other Perks, the Reaper Perk is unlocked at level 14 and currently obtainable through in-game offers.



PERK REMOVER


Yes, you heard right, the feature you’ve been wanting is finally here!


With this new update, you’ll finally be able to remove Perks from a dragon.


Next to each Perk, we’ve added a Trash button. Tap on the button to choose between the two following options:


- Permanently delete all Perks from your chosen Perk slot, using Gold.


or


- Move your Perks to your Storage to be able to reuse them, usingGems



Headover to your Official App Store to update your game and jump into these new features! Hopefully the Grim Reaper won’t come knocking at YOUR door…


(The availability of this update could be subject to slight delays depending on the platform).


And remember, if you want to know more about any specific Perks, and how they work, take a look at our FAQs for more details.
