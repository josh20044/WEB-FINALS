Dragon City’s Storytelling Challenge


OCTOBER 23, 2023




This is the moment lots of our Dragon Masters have been waiting for…

Are you ready to use your imagination and awesome writing skills to create a new Dragon City story?

This time, the story is in your hands! But how?

Participate in the new Dragon City’s Storytelling Challenge and write your own tale using any of the currently available in-game dragons, for a chance to have your very own story be part of the Dragon City universe and receive other in-game rewards.



There are a few rules you need to follow to participate in the Challenge:

The Story must be about one or more already existing Dragon City’s Dragons;

Dragons tied to a third-party promotion (for example, Influencers, YouTubers, or The Walking Dead) won’t be taken into account;

Your Submission must be written in English… But don’t worry, your level of English and your writing skills will not be taken into account; our Writers will give your story the final magical touch to make it perfect;

The most important thing is for you to make your Story original and exciting, and to make sure it connects well with the Dragon City universe;

The Submission must be no more than five hundred (500) words in length. In addition, it is recommended that the Submission is at least one hundred and fifty (150) words in length;

Once your Story is ready, submit it in a PDF or Microsoft Word readable file format and send it to our email: community.competition@socialpoint.es;

It is very important to include your in-game User ID together with your submission (How do you find your User ID? Check this!);

Don’t use any Artificial Intelligence (“AI”) tools to create your Story!



You have 3 weeks to write and submit your Story to our email: the Storytelling Challenge begins at 12:00:01 PM CEST on 23 October 2023 and ends at 11:59:59 AM CET on 13 November 2023.



Once we have received and reviewed all of your Submissions, we will select the 3 winning stories and reward the participants as follows:

The First Winner will receive three hundred (300) Gems; additionally, their Story will be reviewed and readjusted by our Writers to feature in a new Episode of our beloved Heroic Tales;

The Second Winner will receive one hundred and fifty (150) Gems;

The Third Winner will receive seventy-five (75) Gems.

Before participating in the Storytelling Challenge, make sure you are familiar with the full T&Cs – available HERE!,

Good luck, Dragon Masters! Who knows, maybe your ideas will become part of the Dragonverse lore!
