The Eternal Day Event


AUGUST 10, 2023






With temperatures rising and more dragons than ever before populating the Islands, the necessary evil must be done... It’s time to trigger the Eternal
Day.



Meet the first-ever Heroic Eternal dragon: the High Void Eternal Dragon!


From August 10th to October 19th, 2023, participate in the 70-day Eternal Day event to earn amazing rewards, including two Special Skill Skins:


  • Void Skill Skin that deals boosted damage for the Legend and Terra attacks of the High Void Eternal Dragon and grants an Extra Turn!

  • Eternal Skill Skin that deals boosted damage for the Time and Flame attacks of the High Void Eternal Dragon and increases the required threshold to activate its Fatality skill!

THE ETERNAL DAY COLLECTION


Collect Eternal Insignias to complete milestones in the Eternal Day event. For the whole duration of the event, Eternal Insignias can be found in:


  • The next THREE Divine Pass Seasons;

  • 1. The Eternal Day - High Void Eternal Dragon (August 10th -August 24th)

  • 2. It’s Just a Phase - Quantum Phasing Dragon (August 24th - September 21st)

  • 3. Gravity Hurts - Quantum Gravity Dragon (September 21st - October 19th)

  • Upon completing your Divine Pass, you'll find extra missions and nodes to keep earning Divine Points, with which you will be able to claim even more Eternal Insignias! Remember, there are more Insignias found along the Elite Pass!

  • You can also find a new and exclusive offer in the Dragon City Webstore: the Elite Pass PLUS! Purchase the Elite Pass PLUS via the Webstore to receive even more rewards!

  • All of the Heroic Race, Grid, and Maze Event Islands from August 31st to October 23rd

  • Official Dragon City Webstore

  • The Arenas, as additional possible rewards in the Warrior’s Chests

  • Quests and Offers


REWARDS


Participating in the Eternal Day event means you can get tons of rewards! These are the rewards waiting for you at each milestone:


  • Milestone 1 “Collect Eternal Insignias” > Get this Eternal Chest for a chance to earn Gems, Orbs of Eternal Dragons, Eternal Insignias, Food, Breeding Perks, and Habitat Tokens;

  • Milestone 2 “Open gifts from the Void” > Get this Eternal Chest for a chance to earn Orbs of the High Void Eternal Dragon and Orbs of some of the strongest dragons in Dragon City;

  • Milestone 3 “Let the Eternal Day begin…” > Get this Eternal Chest for a chance to earn Gems, Orbs of Eternal Dragons, Eternal Insignias, Food, Breeding Perks, and Habitat Tokens;

  • Milestone 4 “Try out the Void Skin!” > Get this Eternal Chest to obtain 500 Orbs of the High Void Eternal Dragon and its new special Skill Skin, the Void Skill Skin!


SPECIAL DIVINE PASS: THE ETERNAL DAY


(August 10th - August 24th)


Play this special Season of this Divine Pass to obtain the first-ever Heroic Eternal dragon: the High Void Eternal Dragon.


Additionally, by purchasing the Elite Pass, you’ll obtain 300 Orbs of the High Void Eternal Dragon and its special Eternal Skill Skin.


And that’s not all… Treat yourself to the new Webstore-exclusive offer: the Elite Pass PLUS! By purchasing the Elite Pass PLUS, you will receive the Elite Pass, 50 Eternal Insignias, and 40 Gems in-game.



NEW SKILL SKINS


Take your High Void Eternal Dragon to the next level with the Void and Eternal Skill Skins!


  • Void Skill Skin

By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage for its Legend & Terra attacks and also get a powerful Extra Turn skill!


Get the Void Skill Skin by completing the Eternal Day collection.


Please restart your game for this to take effect.



  • Eternal Skill Skin

By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage for its Time & Flame attacks and the required threshold to activate its Fatality skill will increase!


Get the Eternal Skill Skin in the Elite Divine Pass during the Eternal Day Season.


Please restart your game for this to take effect.
