Plasma Purge Event


DECEMBER 28, 2023




The hive mind of the Colony has arrived, and her piercing shrieks can be heard across the horizon... She's fast, loud, and determined to purge the land from life-stealing beasts!

Meet the first-ever Heroic Plasma dragon: the High Harpy Plasma Dragon!

From December 28th, 2023, to March 7th, 2024, participate in the 70-day Plasma Purge event to earn amazing rewards, including two special Skill Skins:

Colony Skill Skin that boosts the damage of the High Harpy Plasma’s Ruling Parasite skill, gives boosted damage to her Dream Attack, and increases the dragon’s life points by 25%!

Hivemind Skill Skin that boosts the damage and received life points of the High Harpy Plasma’s Harpy Howl skill and gives boosted damage to her Time Attack!

Barricade - Barriguard Dragon (January 11th - February 8th)

Upon completing your Divine Pass, you can keep earning Divine Points and claim Plasma Insignias found on the extra node! Remember to choose the Elite Pass to maximize your rewards!

REWARDS

Participating in the Plasma Purge event means you can get tons of rewards! These are the rewards waiting for you at each milestone:

Milestone 1 “Collect Plasma Insignias” > Open this Plasma Chest for a chance to earn Gems, Orbs of Plasma Dragons, Plasma Insignias, Food, and Breeding Perks;

Milestone 2 “Hear Harpy’s Howl” > Open this Plasma Chest for a chance to earn Orbs of the High Harpy Plasma Dragon, Orbs of some of the most powerful dragons in Dragon City, Food, Habitat Tokens, Trading Essences, and Joker Orbs;

Milestone 3 “Join the Hivemind” > Open this Plasma Chest for a chance to obtain the High Harpy Plasma Dragon, Eggs of some of the most powerful dragons in Dragon City, and Gems;

Milestone 4 “Try out the Colony Skill Skin!” > Open this Plasma Chest to obtain 500 Orbs of the High Harpy Plasma Dragon and her new, special Colony Skill Skin

(December 28th - January 11th)

Play this special Season of the Divine Pass to obtain the first-ever Heroic Plasma dragon: the High Harpy Plasma Dragon.

And that’s not all… Treat yourself to the new Webstore-exclusive offer: the Elite Pass PLUS! By purchasing the Elite Pass PLUS, you will receive all the benefits of the Elite Pass, plus 200 extra Orbs of the High Harpy Plasma Dragon!


NEW SKILL SKINS

Take your High Harpy Plasma Dragon to the next level with the Colony and Hivemind Skill Skins!


Colony Skill Skin

By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage for her Ruling Parasite skill & Dream attack and have 25% more life points!

Get the Colony Skill Skin by completing the Plasma Purge Collection.

Please restart your game for this to take effect.


Hivemind Skill Skin

By simply owning this Skin, your dragon will deal boosted damage and receive boosted life points for her Harpy Howl skill and deal boosted damage for her Time attack!

Please restart your game for this to take effect.
